Short Bio

My name is Shu Xinhuan, a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Now, I’m a member of HKUST VisLab with the supervisor, Prof. Qu Huamin. My research interests focus on data visualization and human-computer interaction.

I have got my B.Eng. degree in Zhejiang University with the major of Computer Science and Technology. During this period, I was a member of CAD&CG Lab, supervised by Assistant Professor Yingcai Wu.
