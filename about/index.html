Short Bio

My name is Shu Xinhuan, a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Now, I’m a member of HKUST VisLab with the supervisor, Prof. Qu Huamin. My research interests focus on data visualization and human-computer interaction.

I have got my Bachelor of Engineering degree in Zhejiang University with the main major of Computer Science and Technology. I was also a member of CAD&CG Lab, supervised by Assistant Professor Yingcai Wu. During my undergraduate studies, I minored in Public Administration at Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University.
